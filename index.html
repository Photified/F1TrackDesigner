Track Designer

Draw a world-class racing circuit in a single stroke. Thread the needle through the environment, master the technical flow, and build straights where cars can absolutely floor it.

Circuit Rules

1. Avoid Obstacles: Don't draw through lakes or buildings.

2. Top Speed: Include at least one massive, straight blast so cars can reach top aerodynamic speeds.

3. Flow: Sprawling, complex layouts with tight technical sectors score much higher than simple ovals.
